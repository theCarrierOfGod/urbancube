Frequently Asked Questions

01. Can I withdraw my profit and commissions to Bank?

Yes, you can.

  • Login to your account
  • select the Menu button
  • Select Withdraw profit or Cash-out referrals
  • Select fund wallet or Cash to bank
  • Enter your bank details.
    • 02.How do I fund my wallet?
  • 1. Login to your account
  • Click on the Menu button
  • Select Fund account
  • Enter amount
  • Select payment method
    • 03. What are the payments method available for funding my wallet?
  • Bank Transfer (Automatic confirmation)
  • Bank Transfer ( Manual confirmation)
  • Paystack
  • Airtime to cash funding
    • 04. Can my Customer buy Data directly from the website in my absence?

    YES. Our website is a 3 dimensional website. Your resellers or end users can purchase data in your absence and you will still make your profit. Only Silver and Gold users have access to this function.

    05. How can my resellers by data in my absence?
  • Upgrade your account to Silver or Gold
  • Set up your price list for your resellers
  • Give out your referral link to your customer
  • After registration by your referral, each time they purchase data, based on your price set up for customers, your profit goes to your profit wallet.
    • 06. How do I set up my price list for resellers?
  • Upgrade your account to Silver or Gold
  • Click on the menu button on your account page
  • Click resellers portal
  • Click edit to set up a suitable price list for your resellers
